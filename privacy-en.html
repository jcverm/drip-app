💧 Drip — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Drip application (the "App") handles its users' information.

1. Data we collect

The App does not collect any personal data. All information (enabled topics, progress, notification settings) is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Suggestions and reports

If you use the "Suggest a topic" or "Report an error" feature, your message is sent through the Formspree service to the developer's email address. No personal information other than the content of your message is collected. Formspree has its own privacy policy, available at formspree.io.

3. Notifications

Notifications are handled locally on your device through the native iOS and Android services. No notification data is transmitted to third parties.

4. Advertising

The App currently contains no advertising.

5. Third-party services

The App uses the following services:

  • Expo / EAS — development platform (Expo.dev)
  • Formspree — only when you voluntarily send a suggestion

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify and erase your data. Since we do not collect any personal data, these rights are exercised directly on your device (by deleting the App).

7. Contact

For any question about this privacy policy, contact us at: jc.vermeersch@gmail.com

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of the latest change is shown at the top of this page.

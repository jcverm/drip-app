💧 Drip — Help

Everything you need to get started, and how to reach us if needed.

Getting started

Pick a topic, read the question, think, then reveal the answer. Tell the app whether you knew it: Drip spaces out your reviews so what you learn actually sticks. A few minutes a day is enough.

Create your own cards

In the My Life topic, add your own questions and answers, with a photo if you like. Handy for revising what matters to you, or for a loved one to prepare cards for you.

Get reminders

Turn on notifications in the app settings to review a little every day and keep the habit going.

Language

Drip is bilingual, French and English. The app opens in your phone's language and you can switch anytime in the settings.

Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. Everything stays on your device; nothing is sent to an external server. See our privacy policy.

Is the app paid?

No. Drip is free and contains no advertising.

How do I create my own cards?

In the My Life topic, add your questions, your answers, and a photo if you like.

How do I get reminders?

Turn on notifications in the app settings.

Which languages does the app support?

French and English, based on your phone's language, and changeable in the settings.

I found an error or I have a topic idea.

Use "Report an error" or "Suggest a topic" inside the app, or write to us directly.
